Hello!

I’m Gabriel Vestergaard, a first-year Information Systems student at Brigham Young University. I’m passionate about technology, problem-solving, and using data to drive impactful decisions. I’m currently honing my skills in areas like database design, data analysis, and web development, with a particular interest in how information systems can optimize business processes.

Outside of class, I’ve gained hands-on experience through various roles, such as coaching soccer camps, working as a teacher for kids with special needs, and volunteering for social media marketing. These experiences have shaped my teamwork, leadership, and communication skills—skills that I believe are just as important as technical expertise.

I love playing soccer and used to play professional Futsal I’ve been playing soccer since I was 3 years old, but later in life, I fell in love with futsal — a faster-paced, more technical version of the game. Photography allows me to capture those moments — not just as memories, but as stories I can share. Whether it’s a quiet landscape or a bustling street scene, I find joy in preserving the details that make each place unique. , which allows me to experience and share the world from different perspectives.
